> Why "A mouse chasing a cat" is counterintuitive? Explain.

"A mouse chasing a cat" is counterintuitive because it goes against the typical behavior of both animals. Mice are typically prey animals that avoid cats, while cats are typically predators that hunt mice. The idea of a mouse chasing a cat is unexpected and unusual.
