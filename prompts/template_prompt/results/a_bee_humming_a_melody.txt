> Why "A bee humming a melody" is counterintuitive? Explain.

A bee humming a melody is counterintuitive because bees typically don't produce sounds that would be considered a melody. Bees are more known for producing a buzzing sound, rather than any kind of melodic pattern. Additionally, humming is usually associated with humans and other animals with vocal cords, which bees do not possess.
