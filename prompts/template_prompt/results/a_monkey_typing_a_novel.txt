> Why "A monkey typing a novel" is counterintuitive? Explain.

"A monkey typing a novel" is counterintuitive because it implies that the monkey is able to type with a level of accuracy and proficiency that would be required to write a novel. Monkeys have not been shown to have the cognitive ability to understand the structure and language of a novel, let alone the dexterity to type it.
