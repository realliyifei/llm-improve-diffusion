> Why "A cactus growing in a rainforest" is counterintuitive? Explain.

"A cactus growing in a rainforest" is counterintuitive because cacti are typically found in desert environments. Cacti are adapted to store water and survive in arid climates, whereas rainforests are moist, humid, and have plenty of rainfall. The two environments are very different and it is unlikely that a cactus would be able to survive in a rainforest.
