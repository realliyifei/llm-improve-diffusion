> Why "A whale is jumping over a mountain" is counterintuitive? Explain.

"A whale is jumping over a mountain" is counterintuitive because it is not something that typically happens in nature. Whales are aquatic mammals and they live in the ocean, not on land. Therefore, it would be highly unlikely for a whale to jump over a mountain.
