> Why "A desert covered in snow" is counterintuitive? Explain.

"A desert covered in snow" is counterintuitive because deserts are associated with warm temperatures and dry air, while snow is associated with cold temperatures and wet air. Therefore, the idea of a desert being covered in snow is unexpected and counterintuitive.
